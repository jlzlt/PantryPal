About PantryPal

PantryPal helps you discover, save, and share delicious recipes tailored to your pantry. Our mission is to make home cooking easy, creative, and fun for everyone.

How It Works
1
Generate a recipe:
Enter the ingredients you have on hand in the Home page. PantryPal's smart engine will suggest creative, delicious recipes you can make right now. Use filters to refine your results by time, difficulty, or dietary needs.
2
Save a recipe:
Click the Save button on any recipe you like. Your saved recipes are always available in your personal collection, so you can revisit your favorites anytime.
3
Share to community:
Proud of your creation? Share it with the PantryPal community! Click Share to inspire others and see what recipes are trending in the Shared section.
