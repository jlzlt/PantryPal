About PantryPal

PantryPal helps you discover, save, and share delicious recipes tailored to your pantry.

How It Works:
1
Generate recipes:
  • Enter the ingredients you have on hand, or leave it empty for random suggestions.
  • Use filters to refine your results by time, difficulty, dietary needs, etc.
  • Enter the number of recipes you want.
  • PantryPal's smart engine will suggest creative, delicious recipes you can make right now.
  • Each suggested recipe will be portioned for 1 person. Multiply the ingredients by the number of people you are cooking for.
2
Save a recipe:
Click the Save button on any recipe you like. Your saved recipes are always available in your personal collection, so you can revisit your favorites anytime.
3
Share to community:
Proud of your creation? Share it with the PantryPal community! Click Share to inspire others and see what recipes are trending in the Shared section.
Contact: pantrypal@pantrypal.com
